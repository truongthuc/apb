# Review: Known-Known Workflow Scaling

Date: 2026-07-13

## Scope

Updated APB workflow rules so knowledge coverage and review ceremony scale with task complexity.

## Review Notes

- The owner clarified that known-known work means they know what they are asking for and understand the requirement.
- Small tasks such as typo fixes, text changes, and simple configuration changes should not require all knowledge buckets unless assumptions, risks, or validation concerns actually exist.
- Open Questions are blocking unknowns that require owner input before implementation.
- Assumptions are safe interpretations that can be recorded while work continues.
- Planning Review and Design Review should be explicit for complex or ambiguous work, but the owner's clear request can be sufficient review for small known-known tasks.
- The buckets should expose missing knowledge, not become checklist theater.

## Decision

Accepted as a documentation and template rule update.
